🌟 About Me — Shubham Singh

Hello and welcome! I’m Shubham Singh, a passionate Senior Test Engineer with over 6.5 years of experience in the software industry, specializing in Quality Assurance, Test Automation, and Frontend Testing frameworks. My journey in technology has been a blend of curiosity, persistence, and an ever-growing desire to make software more reliable, scalable, and user-friendly.

I began my professional journey at CSS Corp, where I was introduced to the fundamentals of quality assurance. This early phase shaped my mindset about how every line of code and every user interaction has a story behind it — a story that deserves to work seamlessly. I learned the importance of attention to detail, test coverage, and most importantly, the mindset of thinking like an end user.

Later, during my tenure at Cognizant, I gained exposure to large-scale enterprise systems and global clients. Here, I worked on projects that demanded precision, scalability, and coordination across multi-functional teams. I deepened my expertise in Selenium, Protractor, and TypeScript, automating workflows that reduced manual effort and improved efficiency. It was also here that I started exploring framework design and optimization, which became a major turning point in my career.

Currently, I’m working as a Senior Test Engineer at Alten India Pvt Ltd, where I’ve had the privilege to collaborate with international clients such as Amadeus and Newegg. My role involves end-to-end automation strategy — from framework development to CI/CD integration — using tools and technologies like Playwright, Selenium, Rest Assured, Java, and TypeScript. I enjoy designing automation solutions that not only test effectively but also provide clarity and maintainability for future teams.
